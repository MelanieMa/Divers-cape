Name

DiversCape-Sagres.

Sagres

welcome to a destination with focus on nature. Sagres is a town unlike other in Portugal, being situated in the extreme west of Portugal or as many like to say "The end of Europe.", it is a harbor for people who want to escape to Nature. We have some of the most beautiful beaches, and we offer diving the year around. We offer both entry level courses and advanced technical courses.

Our knowledge

Due to a diverse and dedicated team we have a spread out knowlegde and skill set. With more than 15 years of work experience combined, we are able to offer a various amount of services to our geusts. Besides the wide range of courses you can follow with us, we offer Equipment servicing at an economical price.

Diving in Sagres

Our Dive sites are close to the harbor. Most of our diving is done in Ilhas do Martinhal which is a protected marine area. we also offer various cave diving and we have the possibility to go to Wrecks.

Courses&Prices

Explore diving with us. DiscoverScubaDiving

Do the PADI Discover Scuba Diving and find out all about diving in half a day activity. You will learn the basics and enjoy a sea dive.

*This activity counts towards the OpenWaterDiver Course.

Become a certified diver. OpenWaterDiver

If you want to become an indepentand diver with a certification for life, then do the PADI OpenWaterDiver. This certification will let you go untill 18meters of depth with a buddy and is valid over the world.
